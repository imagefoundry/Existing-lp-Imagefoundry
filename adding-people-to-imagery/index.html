The Brief

Why This

We can add people using AI — and the results are genuinely impressive. But not every method suits every brief. Here's an honest comparison of all five techniques, so you can make the right call from the start.

Five
Methods

01 — 05 Techniques Compared
01 / 05
Method 01

2D Cut-Out
People

Timely & Cheapish, but Tricky

Compared to photography, fast and inexpensive — but often looks unconvincing because lighting, shadows, and perspective rarely match the scene. If you've spotted odd-looking figures in imagery, this is almost certainly why.

Fast Low Cost Often Fake-Looking
Method 02

3D People

Flexible, but Can Look 'CG'

3D models blend naturally into digital scenes — matching lighting, perspective, and shadow perfectly. The main limitation: few truly photoreal models exist. Up close you may notice a subtle polygon effect. Best suited to background figures.

Lighting Match Moderate Cost CG Appearance
02 / 05
03 / 05
★ Recommended Method

Mixed
Methodology

3D Models + AI Editing in Photoshop

This is our go-to for foreground figures. We place 3D people inside the scene for geometrically correct lighting and perspective, then use AI-powered Photoshop tools to make them indistinguishably real — refining faces, skin, and clothing until no one asks questions.

Step 01

Dress the 3D scene with digital people — ensuring accurate lighting, shadows, and perspective from the start.

Step 02

Apply Photoshop AI features — refine facial features, soften CG elements, add photo-like texture to skin and clothing.

Result

Truly believable figures even in the foreground. Proven on our recent project for Optima.

Hybrid Output — Optima Project

Method 04

Classic
Photo Shoot

Accurate, but Expensive and Slow

Professional shoots mapped precisely to your scene by matching exact lighting angle and setup. Beautiful results — but time-consuming and costly, especially across many shots or when updates arise mid-project.

Photorealistic High Cost Slow Turnaround
Method 05

AI-Based
People

Fast, Affordable, and Getting Better

Modern AI generates people instantly, matching scene lighting and context with surprising accuracy. Ideal for lifestyle background figures. Currently limited to lower resolution output — fine for digital and small print, but potentially limiting for A4+ applications.

Fastest Lowest Cost Resolution Limits

What this means for you

Four Things
Worth Knowing

BG ————

Background Figures

For lifestyle background figures — AI, 3D, and the mixed method all deliver outstanding results quickly and affordably.

FG ————

Foreground Focus

For central or hero figures — the hybrid 3D+AI approach is a genuine game-changer, producing believable people that hold up at any scale.

VOL ————

Volume & Speed

For high-volume content — AI workflows make previously slow, expensive processes practical for everyday production.

RES ————

Large Format Print

For A4+ print applications — AI-only may fall short on resolution. Photography or the hybrid method remain the safest choices.
